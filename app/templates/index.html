About

Our meal planner and nutrition tracker app is designed to make managing your meals simple and efficient. Here are some of the key features:

User-Friendly Interface

Enjoy a clean and intuitive UI that makes managing your meals a breeze. The design is straightforward, ensuring that even beginners can navigate effortlessly.

Comprehensive Meal Planning

Easily add, delete, and remove meal items with just a few clicks. Keep track of your inventory with ease, making it simple to stay organized and avoid running out of essentials.

Nutrition Analytics

Track your daily nutrition intake, monitor macronutrients, and visualize your progress over time with our comprehensive analytics tools.

Sharing Capabilities

Share your meal plans and nutritional data with friends, family, or health professionals to get feedback, support, and accountability.

Generate Your Personalized Meal Plan

Creating a meal plan tailored to your needs is simple with NutriMate:

  • Choose from 6 dietary preferences: Keto, Mediterranean, Paleo, Vegan, Vegetarian, or Anything
  • Set your daily calorie target based on your goals
  • Select how many meals you want per day (3-6)
  • Get instant recommendations with detailed nutrition information
  • Don't like a meal? Simply refresh it for new options
